Our Services

Event Stewards

Our event stewards are the friendly and professional face of your event. They assist with guest management, provide directions, and ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for all attendees.

Crowd Control Stewards

Managing large crowds requires expertise and vigilance. Our crowd control stewards ensure smooth movement, prevent overcrowding, and handle any potential disturbances efficiently.

Security Stewards

For events requiring heightened security, our trained security stewards provide access control, monitor for risks, and work alongside security teams to maintain a safe environment.


Hospitality Stewards

Our hospitality stewards enhance the guest experience by providing VIP assistance, managing seating areas, and offering exceptional customer service throughout the event.

Fire & Safety Stewards

Safety is our top priority. Our fire and safety stewards ensure compliance with fire regulations, monitor for hazards, and assist in emergency evacuations if required.

Response Stewards

In high-risk situations, our response stewards are trained to react quickly and professionally. Whether handling crowd disturbances or medical emergencies, they ensure swift and effective action.
